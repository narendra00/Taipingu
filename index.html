Typing speed test

Our typing test helps you to better assess your current abilities and gives you the opportunity to compare your typing speed at different times. This is the basic prerequisite to tap your full potential and see how much time you should invest in your daily typing practice.

Typing Games will make kids forget they are actually getting a keyboarding workout! For younger students its important to practice accuracy, speed and especially the correct typing rhythm.

Typing with vision

A good way to increase your typing speed is to type easy sentences over and over and look entire sentence at first then write. That will help you to type smoothly without pausing. Try taking a typing speed test before and after to see for yourself. You can even work through this section multiple times and then track your progress.
